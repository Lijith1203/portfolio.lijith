Introduction

Hi, I'm Lijith Talluru — a frontend developer and creative designer with a passion for crafting intuitive, visually stunning digital experiences. I love bringing ideas to life through clean, responsive interfaces that combine functionality with aesthetic appeal. What drives me is the challenge of solving real-world problems through design and code, and the satisfaction of seeing users engage with something I've built.

Background and Experience

I hold a B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from Hindustan Institute of Technology and Science, where I built a strong foundation in web technologies, programming, and design thinking.

Over time, I've worked on a variety of projects — from portfolio websites and landing pages to interactive web apps — always aiming to blend creativity with functionality. My experience includes designing responsive layouts, optimizing user experiences, and collaborating with teams to deliver clean, scalable code.

Skills and Expertise

My core expertise lies in frontend development and UI/UX design. I work primarily with HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and frameworks like Tailwind CSS and React. I'm also proficient with design tools such as Figma, which I use to prototype and visualize interfaces before development.

I'm passionate about creating user-friendly experiences, ensuring responsiveness, and maintaining clean, well-documented code. I'm also familiar with Git for version control and have an eye for pixel-perfect design.

Interests and Hobbies

Beyond coding, I enjoy exploring the latest UI trends, sketching interface concepts, and experimenting with new design tools.

I'm a tech enthusiast at heart, always eager to learn and stay updated with emerging web technologies. In my free time, I love listening to music, watching sci-fi content, and getting inspired by the creativity of others in the design and development community.
